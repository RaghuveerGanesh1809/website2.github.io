Aptitude



Percentage 


                Percentages are mathematical problems that require you to calculate and work with percentages. These questions are commonly found in various 
                aptitude tests, entrance exams, and job interviews. They assess your ability to understand and solve problems related to percentages.


Time and Work 


                Time and work are a common type of mathematical problem used in aptitude tests, competitive exams, and job interviews. These questions assess 
                a candidate's ability to solve problems related to the allocation of time and effort to complete a task, often in a work or project setting. 
                The key concepts in time and work aptitude questions include work rates, time, and the relationship between them.


Permutation and Combination 


                Permutation and combination are two fundamental concepts in combinatorics, a branch of mathematics that deals with counting and arranging objects. 
                They are often used to solve problems related to arranging or selecting items from a set.


Probability 


                Probability test your understanding of probability theory and your ability to solve problems related to the likelihood of events occurring. 
                These questions are commonly found in aptitude tests, entrance exams, and assessments for various professions, including statistics, finance,
                and data analysis.


Averages 


                Averages are a common topic in aptitude and mathematical reasoning questions. They involve calculating the mean or average value of a set of 
                numbers or data points. The average is used to find a representative value that summarizes the overall data.
